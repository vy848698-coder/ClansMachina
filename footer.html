Warranty and Return Policy

At Clans Machina, we stand behind the quality and performance of our solar products and services. Our goal is to ensure complete customer satisfaction and reliable energy performance for years to come.

This Warranty and Return Policy explains the terms under which warranty coverage, returns, and replacements are offered for products purchased from Clans Machina.

1 Warranty Coverage

a. Solar Panels

All solar panels sold by Clans Machina come with a performance warranty of up to 25 years (as per manufacturer policy) and a product warranty of 10 years against manufacturing defects.

b. Inverters and Batteries

Inverters and batteries carry a manufacturer warranty ranging from 2 to 5 years, depending on the brand and model purchased.

c. Installation Workmanship

Our installation services are covered under a 1-year workmanship warranty, ensuring that all fittings, mounts, and wiring are safely and professionally installed.

d. Accessories and Balance of System Components

Accessories such as mounting structures, cables, and junction boxes are covered under a 1-year limited warranty from the date of installation.

2 Warranty Exclusions

Warranty will not apply in the following cases:

  • Damage caused by mishandling, misuse, or improper maintenance.
  • Physical damage after delivery (e.g., cracks, water damage, or burns).
  • Unauthorized repair, modification, or relocation of the system.
  • Natural disasters (lightning, flood, fire, earthquake, etc.) or force majeure.
  • Damage due to voltage fluctuations, poor earthing, or external electrical faults.

3 Warranty Claim Process

If you believe your product is defective and falls under warranty:

  • Contact our support team within the warranty period at support@clansmachina.in or call 092373 77503 (India only).
  • Provide the following details:
    • Product serial number and purchase invoice
    • Date of installation and proof of purchase
    • Photos/videos showing the defect or issue
  • Our technical team will inspect and verify your claim.
  • If approved, we will arrange for repair, replacement, or service support based on the manufacturer's warranty terms.

4 Return & Replacement Policy

a. Eligibility for Return

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days from delivery only if the product is found damaged, defective, or incorrect.
  • The product must be unused, in original packaging, and include all manuals and accessories.

b. Non-Returnable Items

  • Installed or site-customized solar systems
  • Products damaged due to user handling or installation errors
  • Clearance or discounted sale items

c. Return Procedure

To initiate a return, contact us at support@clansmachina.in with your order details and reason for return.

If eligible, our team will coordinate pickup or guide you on the return process.

5 Refund Policy

  • Refunds are issued only after inspection and approval of returned goods.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Installation, delivery, or handling charges are non-refundable.
  • In case of part replacement under warranty, only the defective component is replaced — not the full system.

Our total liability under this policy shall not exceed the original purchase price of the product.

Clans Machina shall not be liable for any indirect or consequential losses such as loss of energy production, revenue, or profit.
