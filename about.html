Our Story

Built to Make Solar Simple and Honest

Clans Machina was founded on a straightforward observation: rooftop solar in India was plagued by under-engineered systems, inflated promises and zero after-sales accountability. Customers were sold a one-time installation and then left alone.

We set out to do the opposite. Every system we design is sized around your actual roof, shading pattern and consumption profile — not a copy-paste template. Every install is backed by real-time monitoring and proactive service for the life of the system.

Today, with 12,000+ rooftops powered across 29 cities, we remain obsessed with one metric: how much money our customers actually save, year after year.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

The north star that guides every system we design and every customer we serve.

Our Mission

Make clean, affordable rooftop solar the default choice for every Indian home, business and housing society — delivered with transparent savings, honest engineering and service customers can trust for decades.

Our Vision

An India powered by sunshine — where energy is cost-stable, carbon-light and democratically distributed. We are working toward carbon net-zero contribution by 2040, one rooftop at a time, in every city we serve.

Our Values

The Principles We Build On

Six commitments that shape how we design, install and support every solar system — not marketing copy, but the standards our engineers and service teams are held to.

Radical Transparency

You see the full picture before you commit — line-item pricing, real savings projections and an honest payback timeline, not a glossy estimate.

Engineering Excellence

Every system is designed for your specific roof area, tilt, shading and load profile. We never use one-size templates — real engineering costs less in the long run.

Customer First

Your long-term savings and system uptime are our success metric, not the speed of the next sale. We optimise for your ROI, not ours.

25-Year Accountability

Remote monitoring, preventive maintenance visits and a performance warranty mean we are still accountable to you in year 20, not just at commissioning.

Climate Commitment

Each install is a measurable contribution — we track and report the CO₂ avoided and equivalent trees planted for every system in our fleet.

Proven Reliability

Grade-A panels, Tier-1 inverters, BIS-certified wiring and certified crews. Our 98.4% fleet uptime is not a claim — it is a monitored, published number.
