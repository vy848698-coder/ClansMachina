Our Story

Built to Make Solar Simple and Honest

Clans Machina was founded on a straightforward observation: rooftop solar in India was plagued by under-engineered systems, inflated promises and zero after-sales accountability. Customers were sold a one-time installation and then left alone.

We set out to do the opposite. Every system we design is sized around your actual roof, shading pattern and consumption profile — not a copy-paste template. Every install is backed by real-time monitoring and proactive service for the life of the system.

Today, with 1200+ rooftops powered across 29 cities, we remain obsessed with one metric: how much money our customers actually save, year after year.
