Our Story

Built to Make Solar Simple and Honest

Clans Machina was founded on a simple belief: going solar should be effortless, transparent and genuinely profitable for the customer — not a confusing, one-off sale.

Too many rooftops were being fitted with under-engineered systems and then forgotten. We set out to do the opposite: design every system around the customer's real roof and usage, install it cleanly, and stay accountable for its performance for the next 25 years.

Today we are an engineering-led team delivering rooftop solar for homes, businesses and housing societies across India — backed by subsidy support, real-time monitoring and proactive after-sales service.
